About theproject.

MedSynapse Weekly is a nonprofit, open-access medical newsletter written for students, clinicians and anyone curious about science. Our mission is to make evidence-based medicine accessible — regardless of language, institution or geography.

Every article is written by our editorial team and published simultaneously in English, German and Italian. We accept no advertising and receive no industry funding.

We are always looking for readers who want to participate — ask questions, suggest topics, or contribute a piece.
